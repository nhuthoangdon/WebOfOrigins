About the Data Visualisation

Reading the Network

The network visualises connections between consumer products, their direct ingredients/compositions, raw materials, industries, and resulting social/environmental impacts, together with the associated countries in hierarchical order. Use the control panel on the right of the network canvas to find keywords and toggle views (e.g., show/hide countries). Nodes represent entitites; edges show relationships. Hover over nodes and edges to see additional info.

Network Structure

Nodes are color-coded by hierarchy level

• Country: countries associated with the end products (Item nodes)
• Item: end products (e.g. "Jello"): Blue – Starting points for consumer goods. iCountry (item-related countries): Gray (#DFE2E5) – Prominent producers/importers/exporters of items. Composition (e.g., ingredients like "Gelatin"): Purple (#C869ED) – Breakdown of item components. Source (e.g., "Animal Byproducts"): Blue (#6591FF) – Origins of compositions. rawMaterial (e.g., "Chitin"): Brown (#CEA372) – Base materials extracted from sources. smCountry (source/material countries): Gray (#DFE2E5) – Key countries for sources/materials. Industry (e.g., "Shrimp Farming"): Teal (#60A4C2) – Production processes.
