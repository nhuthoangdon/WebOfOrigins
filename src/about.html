The Web of Origins Project

Most of today’s consumer products—from coffee and medications to household items—are the result of complex industrial processes. Many of their materials, ingredients, or compositions share common origins, intertwine across sectors and industries, or lead to similar impacts.

Web of Origins (WOO) is an ambitious project dedicated to mapping the intricate network of interconnected industries. It catalogs a wide range of products and their sources, exploring the entangled web of industrialisation. The project also seeks to reveal how products and industries are directly or indirectly linked to social and environmental impacts—from ethical concerns and human well-being to ecological sustainability.

By making these connections visible, WOO empowers people to make informed choices and encourages a deeper understanding of the world’s interconnected industrial systems and supply chains.

weboforigins.com is committed to providing general, unbiased, evidence-backed data, ensuring transparency and accuracy through current and available information. While still in its early stages, the database will continue to grow and expect ongoing expansion and improvment over time.
